3D Rasterizer

3D rasterizer written in C++ and QT creator. Implemented Lambertian, Blinn Phong, and toon shading.

To rasterize a single triangle (first in 2D space), I used pixel-row overlap and also wrote a function to get the barycentric influence of the triangle's 3 points, given a point within the triangle. The z-coordinates were sorted on a per-fragment basis rather than a per-triangle basis. This is so that shapes in 3D space are sorted appropriately.

I also implemented a Camera class to generate view projection matrices and perspective projection matrices. This class contains member variables defining the X, Y, and Z axis, as well as values for the field of view, near and far clip plane, position, and aspect ratio.

FEATURES

  • Texture mapping: Using the vertices' UV coordinates, I mapped texture colors to the geometry with barycentric interpolation.
  • Lambertian reflection: I used the Lambertian lighting model to give the model a bit of lighting, with our Camera object's look vector as the light direction.
