About

・ I'm Raymond Feng, a sophomore studying Digital Media Design at the University of Pennsylvania.

・ I was born in Toronto, Canada, but I grew up in Warrington, Pennsylvania.

・ My background is mostly in game development and digital art, but I'm exploring computer graphics and web development.

・ I love creating things, whether it be illustrations, websites, or games! I have a passion for learning new skills and making innovative applications.

・ My other interests include running, comics and manga, animation, and marine life. Octopuses are my favorite animal!
